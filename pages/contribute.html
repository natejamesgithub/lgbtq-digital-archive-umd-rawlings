Community Contributions

Share Your Story, Safely and Respectfully

This archive is built with the community, for the community. Before submitting, please review the guidelines below to protect your rights, the rights of others, and the long-term integrity of the archive.

Ways to Contribute

  • Oral histories (audio recordings or transcripts)
  • Photographs and visual documentation
  • Personal narratives and reflections
  • Community event documentation
  • Historical materials (flyers, posters, records)

Ready to submit? Use the Admin Upload page (for authorized contributors), or contact the project team to coordinate a community submission.

Consent & Permission (Required)

You must have permission to submit all content. By contributing, you confirm that:

  • You are the creator, OR you have permission from the creator
  • You have consent from anyone identifiable in the material
  • You have the right to share and publish this content publicly

DC, Maryland, and Virginia all recognize rights of privacy and publicity. Submitting content without permission may violate those rights.

Minors (Under 18)

Content involving minors requires special care:

  • Parental or guardian consent is required
  • No sensitive or identifying information without permission
  • No exploitative or harmful content will be accepted

Submissions involving minors may be removed if consent cannot be verified.

Copyright & Ownership

You retain ownership of your content. By submitting, you grant the archive a non-exclusive license to:

  • Display your content publicly
  • Preserve it as part of the archive
  • Use it for educational and research purposes

Do not upload copyrighted material unless you have permission or it is clearly in the public domain.

Privacy & Sensitive Content

LGBTQ+ histories can involve sensitive and personal information. Please:

  • Avoid outing individuals without explicit consent
  • Be mindful of safety, especially for vulnerable communities
  • Consider anonymizing details when appropriate

We reserve the right to remove or restrict content that poses a safety or privacy risk.

Content Standards

Submissions must not include:

  • Hate speech or harassment
  • Explicit threats or incitement to violence
  • Non-consensual or exploitative material
  • False or misleading historical claims

Takedown Requests

If you believe content violates your rights, you may request removal.

  • Include a link to the content
  • Explain your relationship to the material
  • Provide supporting documentation if applicable

We will review requests in good faith and remove content when appropriate.

Contact the Project Team

Questions about contributing, permissions, takedown requests, or community partnerships? Reach out to the project team.
